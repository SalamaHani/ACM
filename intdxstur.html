The Deeper The Roots, The Higher You Can Reach.

From Center City to Atlantic City.
From Middletown to the Main Line. From five offices to more than seventy-five. From 1886 to this moment…

We support people who reach for what’s next. In their careers, in their communities, in their lives.

We are Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices Fox & Roach, REALTORS and The Trident Group

Everything You Need

Here, you’re never on your own — in a transaction, a challenging situation, or your next career move. We train you on what matters — how to serve and protect, interpret changing markets, navigate challenges, and use the latest technology to your benefit.

A Unified Team

Here, you’re never alone. You, and your clients, can move with confidence knowing you have a unified team — brokerage staff and managers, mortgage, title and insurance professionals — working together to achieve a single goal: yours.

A Track Record Without Equal

135 years has given us more than just legacy. It’s given us a deep network of relationships with an authentic connection to the communities we serve. It is that connection that drives our initiatives for giving back.
